
# Crestwood and Emprise du lion

- In Crestwood, perform a WMG and go to Emprise du Lion. You want to hover your mouse in this location on the loading screen to click it the moment it shows up, press space to travel.
- In Emprise, do WMG and spam spacebar as the save loads. This opens up the local map with all markers.
- Travel to keep and capture. Travel back to camp
- Grab dawnstone that is down the hill
- Travel to skyhold
